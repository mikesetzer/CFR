Retirement Plan Consulting Program (RPCP)

The RPCP program is a division of LPL Financial LLC, our broker/dealer. Through this program we can partner with you as a 3(21) advisor or assume your ERISA fiduciary responsibilities as a 3(38) investment advisor. LPL Financial provides us with the tools and resources needed to support our plan sponsors.

Center for Fiduciary Excellence (CEFEX)

CEFEX is an independent global assessment and certification organization dedicated to helping investment advisors and managers apply the highest standards of fiduciary excellence in the investment process, governance, risk management and operational processes.

Retirement Plan Operation’s Manual

We created the “Operations Manual” as a detailed guide for our plan sponsors and plan trustees. The manual includes information regarding your ERISA fiduciary responsibilities and preparation for Department of Labor Audits, as well as, all of your plan documents and investment reports and details. It is designed to guide and help you minimize your fiduciary risk.

Accredited Investment Fiduciary (AIF)

An AIF Designation is offered by the Center for Fiduciary Studies and provides advisors with the opportunity to enhance their fiduciary knowledge. Our clients benefit from using an advisor with this accreditation because we are held to a standard of excellence to which others may not adhere.
