Chairvolotti Financial Client Gateway

Our Client Gateway allows plan participants access to weekly market analysis and fund performance data.

Plan Checker

Plancheck’r is an integrated collaborative software used by Plan Sponsors to ensure continual compliance of their retirement plans. It is a comprehensive tool to help Plan Sponsors better understand and mitigate their fiduciary duties.

Finance Logix

Finance Logix is financial planning software that combines the latest technologies with easy to use financial planning tools.

Morningstar

Morningstar provides stock market analysis, equity, mutual fund, and ETF research, ratings, and portfolio tools. It is also a good source for IRA, 401(k) and 529 plan research.

fI360 Toolkit

The fI360 Toolkit is web-based software that provides analytical, management and reporting features needed to help investment professionals manage and document a prudent investment process. It promotes compliance and provides users with better information.

LPL Account View

Account View provides convenient 24-hour online access to your LPL Investment account, including, statements, tax documents and trade confirmations. You also have access to current market data.

Dorsey Wright & Associates (DWA) Team Builder

DWA is a registered investment advisory firm based in Richmond VA. They offer extensive investment research and analysis. They use Point & Figure Charting, Relative Strength Analysis and numerous other tools to analyze market data.

FiRM – Center for Fiduciary Management (CFFM)

CFFM specializes in providing the retirement plan industry with investment monitoring services through its toolset. FiRM helps to determine whether the objectives are being met as stated in the Investment Policy Statement with regard to the plan investments. It also documents the analysis and basis for certain decisions made.
